# DeepTechly Content Templates

## Tone Rules

DeepTechly writing should be:

- Clear
- Analytical
- Calm
- Institutional
- Technically literate
- Investor-aware
- Evidence-driven
- Careful with uncertainty
- Direct

Avoid:

- Hype
- Exaggeration
- Unsupported claims
- Casual startup slang
- Empty buzzwords
- Overconfident technical conclusions
- Fake precision

## Required Claim Language

Use:

```txt
Public sources indicate...
The company states...
Available evidence suggests...
DeepTechly could not confirm...
No public source was found for...
This appears to...
This may indicate...
```

Avoid:

```txt
The company will...
This proves...
Guaranteed...
Revolutionary...
Unmatched...
Definitive...
```

## Writing Quality Standard

Every generated page should answer:

1. What is this?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What is technically different?
4. What evidence supports it?
5. What remains uncertain?
6. What is the investor or institutional read?
7. What should be watched next?
